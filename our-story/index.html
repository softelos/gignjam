{{site.title}} was launched in early 2016. Although still at its infancy, we have a mission. Our mission is to be the hub for music lovers to network and collaborate, inspire anyone with a passion to pursue their dreams and be recognized for their work.

Gig n Jam provides the tools and resources to connect people, places and events in the music industry.

We dedicate our work to Sandra, a vibrant teenager with a passion for music. Sandra has been fighting brain cancer since 2014. She is recovering and looks forward to singing and playing the piano again.

Get well soon!. You're an inspiration to us all!
