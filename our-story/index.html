We are a global online music community that connects people, places and events in the music industry. We believe that music is the language of love that bridge people all over the world regardless of their culture, background and language spoken.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the hub for independent musicians, managers, labels and music fans to network,collaborate, and to be recognized for their work. We strive to be the leader and the voice supporting music culture and independent music communities worldwide.

Our Goals

~ Create awareness and revival of music culture worldwide to inspire anyone with a passion for music to pursue their dream ~

~ Increase the quality and quantity of original music for all types of genre ~

~ Support philanthropy for music ~

For Fans

Musicians need Fans like you. Anyone can be a fan. Sign up with us and take the opportunity to discover new music and musicians and find the next breakout hit and talent. Fans can also use our Directory to find and book local musicians to perform live music at their wedding, private parties or company events. We strive to provide extra perks for our fan club members.

For Musicians

Our goal is to provide support and promote unsigned and independent musicians to be recognized for their work and earn income that can sustain their music career full time. We help serious musicians to launch and grow their music career by providing musicians an outlet to promote, perform and be discovered by new fans and record labels.

For Venues

Our goal is to provide venues the opportunity to drive traffic to their events. We provide a directory to find and book talented musicians and also a platform to manage and promote your events and shows. Get listed in our directory and be found by fans and musicians.

For Businesses

Businesses in the music industry will be listed in our directory. We strive to provide businesses the opportunity to promote their businesses and drive traffic and sales to their stores. We are working towards a marketplace to buy and sell all things music.
