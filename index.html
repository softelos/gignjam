What do you get as a {{site.title}} member?

Many tools and different levels of access to satisfy the needs of the music industry

{{site.title}} Directory

The {{site.title}} Directory is a search tool that let fans, musicians, promoters and other bands search our network of musicians, narrow results by location, genre and statistics, directly message bands and book the talent they're looking for.

Integrated Messaging System

Using {{site.title}} Messaging you can communicate with fans, promoters, and other musicians to develop your professional network, manage your band and book more shows.

Unmatched network of opportunities

Browse and apply to a wide array of music opportunities listed by our network of thousands of promoters seamlessly through the Sonicbids booking platform.

My Social Calendar

Find concerts, festivals, local events. Find local gigs and discover new and emerging artists and performers. Create your event, post details about your event and share it with friends.

Gig booking platform

The {{site.title}} gig booking platform is the industry leading way for your band to apply to gigs, book shows and manage your calendar and makes booking shows as simple as clicking a button.

Artist Metrics

Your social statistics from Facebook, Twitter, Soundcloud, and Youtube are included in your Sonicbids EPK to help your submissions stand out against other gig applicants.
