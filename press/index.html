What is {{site.title}}?

GignJam is the global online music community that connects people, places and events in the music industry. Our mission is to be the hub for independent musicians, managers, labels and music fans to network, collaborate, and to be recognized for their work.

We strive to create awareness and revival of music culture worldwide to inspire anyone with a passion for music to pursue their dream.

We also want to increase the quality and quantity of original music for all types of genre and support philanthropy for music.
